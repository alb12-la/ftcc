About Us

FUL Treasures Community Center is a grassroot organization with a pending 501c3 status. The word FUL is an acronym for Faith Uplifting Love reflecting out core principles. FUL Treasures Community Center mission is to break the cycle of violence, and intergenerational trauma in urban communities by addressing the whole person through mental and social services. FUL Treasures Community Center inspires to build our communities by utilizing a holistic approach providing mental health services, physical health, social services, extracurricular activities, creative arts, and educational support. FUL Treasures Community Center will provide a safe space for all Southside of Chicago communities to prosper, grow, unify, and thrive.
